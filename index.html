ABOUT US

Sexual harassment is a form of illegal sex discrimination involving unwelcome sexual advances, requests for sexual favors, and other verbal or physical conduct that is based on sex. All forms of sexual harassment are illegal, and individuals may be liable for this behavior if they do not take the proper steps to address it.
